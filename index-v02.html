About Me
I am a special faculty in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. I lead the Retail Analytics, Intelligence & Sustainability Excellence (RAISE) Initiative in CMU. Prior to this, I was with the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at CMU as a research scientist. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from CMU in 2016.

Research
My research interests include energy system optimization and control, distributed computations, smart grid, and electrified transportation systems.
